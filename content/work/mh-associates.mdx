## The problem

MH Associates had an outdated company presence that undersold a serious business.
For an EPC firm, credibility is the whole pitch — the site needed to look the part
and make it easy for prospective clients to get in touch.

## Key decisions

**I led with proof, not services.** For contracting work, buyers trust what you've
already delivered, so the structure puts real projects and capability front and
centre and routes everything toward an enquiry.

**I kept an image-heavy gallery fast.** A project gallery is the most persuasive
part of the site and the easiest to make slow, so imagery was optimized so it
loads quickly on any connection.

## What I built

A responsive marketing website with clear conversion paths, a services showcase, an
optimized project gallery, a downloadable company profile, and a floating contact
widget — built from reusable components and deployed to production.

## The outcome

A credible, fast presence that matches the quality of the business and gives
prospective clients an easy, obvious way to make contact.

## Built with

React, Vite, and Tailwind CSS.
