## The problem

Mavin Star needed a trustworthy online presence that encourages donations and
volunteer participation — without any backend infrastructure to maintain or pay
for. For a small non-profit, running costs and complexity matter as much as looks.

## Key decisions

**I chose boring, durable technology on purpose.** A fully static build means
nothing to break, nothing to patch, and near-zero hosting cost — exactly what a
volunteer-run organisation can sustain long-term.

**I made the two actions that matter impossible to miss.** Everything routes toward
donating or volunteering, so a visitor who wants to help always has a clear next
step.

## What I built

A lightweight, responsive website with a program showcase, clear donation and
volunteer flows, a custom carousel, and structured content — built with reusable
components and deployed as a fully static site.

## The outcome

A fast, trustworthy presence the organisation can keep running with almost no
technical overhead, that makes helping out straightforward.

## Built with

React, Vite, and React Router.
